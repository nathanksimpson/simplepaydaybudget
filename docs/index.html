How it works (Conservative Logic)

This version treats your payday as the final day of your current budget. This is safer if you don't receive your paycheck until late in the day.

Logic: If your balance is $1000 and payday is in 10 days (counting today and payday), your allowance is ($1000 - bills) / 10 days.

Bills: Any bill due on your payday is deducted from your current balance to ensure you don't accidentally spend that money before the bill hits.
